🗺️ 魔法学院探索攻略 Hogwarts Exploration Guide
🎓 欢迎来到霍格沃茨！ 🎓 Welcome to Hogwarts!

你收到了霍格沃茨的录取通知书！现在开始你的魔法之旅吧！

  • 🎩 第一步：分院帽测试 - 访问 分院帽 页面，测试你的所属学院（格兰芬多、斯莱特林、拉文克劳、赫奇帕奇）
  • 📚 第二步：对角巷购物 - 在 对角巷 购买魔法用品，包括魔杖、长袍、课本等
  • 🏰 第三步：进入学院 - 来到 霍格沃茨城堡，开始你的学习生活
  • 🧙 第四步：选择课程 - 在 课程页面 选择你感兴趣的魔法课程
  • 🌍 第五步：世界语言课 - 在 世界语言课 学习基本的语言技能
  • 📖 第六步：知识闯关 - 在 知识闯关 页面，测一测你对哈利·波特魔法宇宙的熟悉度

你还可以在巫师营地，查看金加隆排行榜和玩法攻略，在巫师留言板写下你的心情、感想和困惑，或者完成巫师任务公告栏的每日任务获取额外奖励，在魔法导师专区查看老师对你的好感度，还可以随时翻阅预言家日报。有空时可以在角色聊天页面找哈利、罗恩和赫敏聊聊天。

You've received your Hogwarts acceptance letter! Begin your magical journey!

  • 🎩 Step 1: Sorting Hat Test - Visit Sorting Hat to discover your house (Gryffindor, Slytherin, Ravenclaw, Hufflepuff)
  • 📚 Step 2: Diagon Alley Shopping - Buy magical supplies at Diagon Alley (wand, robes, textbooks)
  • 🏰 Step 3: Enter the Academy - Arrive at Hogwarts Castle to start your studies
  • 🧙 Step 4: Choose Classes - Select your favorite magic courses on the Classes page
  • 🌍 Step 5: World Languages - Learn basic language skills at World Languages
  • 📖 Step 6: Knowledge Quiz - Test your knowledge of Harry Potter universe at Knowledge Quiz

You can also visit Wizard Camp to check the Galleon Leaderboard and play guides, write your thoughts on the Wizard Message Board, complete daily tasks on the Wizard Quest Board for extra rewards, check teacher affection in the Magical Mentors section, and read the Daily Prophet. When you have time, you can chat with Harry, Ron, and Hermione in the Character Chat page.

✨ 快速成长小贴士 ✨ Quick Growth Tips

💡 每日必做：每天登录可获得免费精力重置，记得完成至少1-2门课程
💡 精力管理：精力值快用完时学习单词卡片（不消耗精力），提前在三把扫帚买好恢复食品
💡 好感度提升：连续完成课程可获得额外好感度，避免频繁失败
💡 金币最大化：优先完成飞行课和魔药课，保持连胜可获得额外奖励
💡 成就解锁：在个人资料页查看"徽章展示墙"，完成课程可解锁精美徽章和称号

💡 Daily Must-Do: Log in daily for free energy reset, complete 1-2 classes
💡 Energy Management: Study flashcards when energy is low (no cost), stock up on recovery foods
💡 Affection Boost: Complete classes consecutively, avoid frequent failures
💡 Max Galleons: Prioritize Flying and Potions classes, maintain winning streaks
💡 Achievements: Check "Badge Display Wall" in profile, unlock badges and titles

⚡ 精力值系统 Energy System
⚡ 精力值规则 ⚡ Energy Rules
  • ⏰ 初始精力：60点，最大值100点
  • ⏳ 衰减速率：每分钟减少1点，离线15分钟后暂停衰减
  • 🍺 恢复方式：三把扫帚商店购买食品恢复
  • 🌙 每日重置：每天重置到60点
  • ⚠️ 课程要求：需要≥10点精力才能参加课程
  • ⏰ Initial: 60 points, Max: 100 points
  • ⏳ Decay: 1 point/min, stops after 15min offline
  • 🍺 Recovery: Purchase food at Three Broomsticks
  • 🌙 Daily Reset: Back to 60 points each day
  • ⚠️ Requirement: ≥10 points to attend classes
🪙 金加隆获取规则 Galleon Earning Rules
💡 金加隆获取方式 💡 Galleon Earning Methods
  • 🎓 完成课程：每门课程根据表现奖励5-100金币
  • ⏱️ 学习单词卡片：学习满15分钟奖励30金币
  • 📝 单词测验：根据测验得分奖励1-50金币
  • 📜 完成任务：在巫师任务公告栏完成每日任务获得额外奖励
  • ⚡ 特殊活动：参与限时活动获得额外奖励
  • 🎓 Complete Classes: 5-100 Galleons per class based on performance
  • ⏱️ Study Flashcards: 30 Galleons for 15 minutes of study
  • 📝 Word Quiz: 1-50 Galleons based on quiz scores
  • 📜 Complete Quests: Daily quests on the Wizard Quest Board for extra rewards
  • ⚡ Special Events: Limited-time events offer extra rewards

❤️ 好感度系统 Affection System
💕 好感度规则 💕 Affection Rules

共有9位导师，每位对应不同课程，好感度影响角色态度和特殊剧情：

  • 🎓 0-9分：低 - "还需要更多互动"
  • 🎓 10-19分：一般 - （无提示）
  • 🎓 20-49分：中 - "对你印象不错"
  • 🎓 ≥50分：高 - "非常欣赏你！"

9 teachers, each for different classes. Affection affects character attitudes and special storylines:

  • 🎓 0-9 points: Low - "Need more interaction"
  • 🎓 10-19 points: Average - (No hint)
  • 🎓 20-49 points: Medium - "Good impression"
  • 🎓 ≥50 points: High - "Highly appreciated!"
🏆 成就系统 Achievement System
🎖️ 成就与徽章 🎖️ Achievements & Badges

完成课程和挑战可解锁30个精美成就，每个成就对应一个称号和一款徽章：

  • 🏆 未获得：灰色徽章，无特效
  • ⭐ 已获得：彩色徽章，带有微光动画
  • 📜 称号展示：每个成就对应独特称号
  • 🏰 查看方式：在个人资料页点击"徽章展示墙"按钮

Complete classes and challenges to unlock 30 beautiful achievements, each with a title and badge:

  • 🏆 Unlocked: Gray badge, no effects
  • ⭐ Obtained: Colored badge with glowing animation
  • 📜 Titles: Each achievement has a unique title
  • 🏰 View: Click "Badge Display Wall" in profile
