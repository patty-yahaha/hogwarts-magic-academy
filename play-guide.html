⚡ 精力值系统 Energy System
⚡ 什么是精力值？ ⚡ What is Energy?

精力值是你在魔法世界的行动力。每次进行课程或活动都会消耗精力值，精力值耗尽后需要通过购买食品恢复才能继续。

Energy is your action power in the magical world. Each class or activity consumes energy. When energy runs out, you must purchase food to recover it.

📊 精力值规则 📊 Energy Rules
  • ⏰ 初始精力：100点
  • 🍺 恢复方式：只能通过三把扫帚商店购买食品恢复
  • 🍔 恢复规则：不同食品恢复不同数量的精力值
  • ⏳ 自然衰减：精力值会随游玩时间不断减少
  • 🌙 每日重置：每天精力值会在0点重置
  • ⏰ Initial Energy: 100 points
  • 🍺 Recovery Method: Only by purchasing food at Three Broomsticks
  • 🍔 Recovery Rule: Different foods restore different amounts of energy
  • ⏳ Natural Decay: Energy decreases over time during gameplay
  • 🌙 Daily Reset: Energy resets at midnight every day
💡 省力小技巧 💡 Energy Tips

合理安排游戏时间，精力值快用完时选择学习单词卡片，既不消耗精力又能积累学习时间！记得提前储备一些恢复食品！

Plan your gaming time wisely. When energy is low, study flashcards - it doesn't consume energy and accumulates study time! Remember to stock up on recovery foods in advance!

🌟 好感度有什么用？ 🌟 What is Affection For?

好感度会影响角色在聊天中的回复态度。好感度越高，角色越友好！解锁特殊剧情需要达到一定好感度。

Affection affects character attitudes in chat. Higher affection means friendlier responses! Special storylines require certain affection levels.
